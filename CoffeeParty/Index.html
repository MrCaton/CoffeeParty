Who are we?

Sturbacks is a company created in 2020. It was created in Stockholm in the neighborhood of Sodermalm. There a group of coffee experts after 20 years sharing great moments drinking "Fika" decided to start a new chain of low-cost coffee shops.

What do we do?

We choose the most select coffee beans and use new technologies to make a grind that keeps all the essence of the coffee. Afterwards, we store the ground coffee maintaining a constant temperature so that the product is not damaged. In our coffee stores we use the latest technology coffee makers, extracting all the flavor from our grinders.

Why us?

Although we are a young company our employees are among the most experienced in the field. We are also the first company to join a technology company with one of our favorite drinks, coffee.

Coffee party, that's how it works!

The coffee parties are divided into Individuals and Business. In both parties you will enjoy our best coffees accompanied by a selection of foods, generally sweet, that will improve our coffee experience. Coffee will be tasted in its different forms, although more emphasis will be given to espresso.
